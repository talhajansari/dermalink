About Us

The SkinCheck platform was designed to simplify the process of receiving a dermatological opinion.

In some cities, it can take up to 72 days to see a dermatologist. Through the SkinCheck platform, you are able receive professional opinions instantly. Snap a photo of the area of concern, and it is sent through an encrypted network to top-tier dermatologists. The platform is designed to provide you with possible diagonoses generated by crowd-sourcing to doctors. Your photos and information will never be shared with anyone outside of our network of doctors.
